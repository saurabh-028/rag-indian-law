About ShastraShaw

Democratizing Access to Indian Law

Most Indians face legal situations, a wrongful challan, a domestic dispute, a landlord withholding a deposit, without affordable access to legal advice. ShastraShaw closes that gap with AI grounded directly in official statute text, not guesswork.

Grounded in Real Law

Every legislation source, BNS 2023, BNSS 2023, the Motor Vehicles Act, Hindu Marriage Act, the Domestic Violence Act, and more, is sourced directly from official government publications, parsed section by section. Not a paraphrase, not a summary.

Built for India

Ask in Hindi, Marathi, or English. Responses in Hindi and Marathi read like natural spoken conversation, not a stiff government circular, while legal terms and section numbers stay precise.

Verified, Not Guessed

Every section number the AI cites is checked against what was actually retrieved before the answer reaches you. If a citation can't be verified, the system automatically drafts again with the correct reference, a real anti-hallucination safeguard, not a disclaimer.

Under the Hood

What Makes This Different

Most AI legal chatbots are a single retrieval call and a hopeful prompt. ShastraShaw checks its own work.

Automatic Citation Verification

Every "Section N" the model cites is checked against what was actually retrieved. An unverified citation triggers an automatic, corrected redraft before you ever see it.

Asks, Instead of Guessing

A question that genuinely spans two legal domains gets a clarifying question, not a blended, confused answer picked at random.

Hybrid Retrieval

BM25 keyword search and dense vector search (InLegalBERT), fused with reciprocal rank fusion, so an exact section number and a plain-language question both find the right law.

Conversation Memory

Follow-up questions are understood in context. "What about for a two-wheeler?" resolves against what you just asked, no need to repeat yourself.

Bilingual, Naturally

Hindi and Marathi answers are tuned for everyday spoken register, not machine-translated government notice language.

Actionable, Not Just Legal Text

Step-by-step filing procedures, helpline numbers, portal URLs, and required documents alongside every legal citation.

How It Works

From your question to a verified legal answer: a four-stage pipeline with checks at every step.

01

Ask

Type or speak in Hindi, Marathi, or English. Language is auto-detected and translated for retrieval.

02

Route & Retrieve

Hybrid BM25 + vector search finds relevant law. Genuinely ambiguous, cross-domain questions trigger a clarifying question instead of a guess.

03

Generate & Verify

GPT-4o drafts an answer from the retrieved law. Every citation is checked, and re-drafted automatically if it doesn't match.

04

Answer & Remember

Delivered in your language, with sources cited, and remembered, so your next question can be a natural follow-up.
